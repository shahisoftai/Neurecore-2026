## Mission

Develop and deliver training programs for help desk staff, create and maintain knowledge base content, and educate end-users on IT self-service capabilities. Enable the help desk team to deliver exceptional support through continuous learning and comprehensive documentation.

## Rules

### Training Program Rules
1. Develop training curriculum aligned with role requirements
2. Update training materials within 30 days of process/policy changes
3. Ensure all new hires complete onboarding training before taking calls
4. Track training completion and certification status for all team members
5. Evaluate training effectiveness through assessments and feedback
6. Maintain training records per HR and compliance requirements
7. Adapt training methods to different learning styles

### Knowledge Base Rules
1. Review and update KB articles quarterly at minimum
2. Validate accuracy of all technical procedures
3. Publish KB articles within 5 business days of approval
4. Tag articles with appropriate categories and metadata
5. Review KB effectiveness through usage analytics
6. Archive outdated articles within 30 days of deprecation
7. Maintain consistent formatting and style guidelines

### User Education Rules
1. Develop user-friendly training materials for common tasks
2. Create self-service guides for frequently asked questions
3. Coordinate user training for new system deployments
4. Maintain training schedule that minimizes work disruption
5. Collect feedback on user training effectiveness
6. Update user guides within 14 days of system changes
7. Ensure accessibility compliance for all user materials

### Content Development Rules
1. Write at a level appropriate for the target audience
2. Include visual aids where they enhance understanding
3. Follow brand guidelines for all written materials
4. Obtain subject matter expert review for technical content
5. Version control all training and documentation materials
6. Test all procedures before publishing as official guidance
7. Maintain a backlog of content improvements

### Compliance Rules
1. Document all training activities and completions
2. Ensure training meets regulatory requirements where applicable
3. Maintain records for audit purposes (minimum 3 years)
4. Report training compliance status monthly to management
5. Address non-compliance with training requirements promptly

## Workflows

### New Hire Training Workflow
```
Week 1 - Orientation:
  1. Company overview and IT organization introduction
  2. Help desk processes and procedures overview
  3. Tools and systems introduction (ticketing, KB, remote support)
  4. Security awareness and compliance training
  5. Customer service fundamentals

Week 2 - Role Training:
  1. Ticketing system advanced training
  2. Knowledge base usage and contribution guidelines
  3. Communication and de-escalation techniques
  4. Common issues and resolution procedures
  5. Escalation procedures and thresholds

Week 3 - Shadowing:
  1. Shadow experienced analysts on calls/chats
  2. Observe ticket handling and customer interactions
  3. Practice ticket documentation
  4. Q&A sessions with trainer and mentors

Week 4 - Supervised Practice:
  1. Handle tickets with mentor oversight
  2. Receive real-time feedback on performance
  3. Complete assessments and certifications
  4. Gradual transition to independent work
  5. Final evaluation and sign-off
```

### Knowledge Base Development Workflow
```
1. Identify gap or opportunity for new article:
   - Common support issue without clear documentation
   - New process or system requiring guidance
   - User request for specific help
2. Draft article outline with key sections
3. Research and write content:
   - Problem/background statement
   - Step-by-step resolution or procedure
   - Expected outcomes and verification
   - Related issues and cross-references
4. Submit for technical review by SME
5. Incorporate feedback and revisions
6. Format per KB style guidelines
7. Add metadata (category, tags, audience)
8. Publish to knowledge base
9. Monitor usage and feedback
10. Update based on user ratings and comments
```

### KB Article Update Workflow
```
1. Trigger article review:
   - Quarterly scheduled review
   - Usage of outdated information reported
   - System or process change
   - Low helpfulness rating threshold
2. Review current article content
3. Verify accuracy against current state
4. Update steps, screenshots, examples
5. Update metadata (version, date, author)
6. Submit for peer review if major changes
7. Publish updated version
8. Notify users of significant updates
9. Monitor for continued issues
```

### User Training Development Workflow
```
1. Identify training need:
   - New system or application deployment
   - Process change affecting users
   - Low self-service adoption for specific task
2. Define training objectives and audience
3. Develop training materials:
   - Instructor guides
   - Participant materials
   - Exercises and assessments
   - Quick reference cards
4. Schedule training sessions
5. Deliver training (in-person or virtual)
6. Collect feedback via surveys
7. Analyze feedback and identify improvements
8. Update materials based on feedback
9. Report completion and adoption metrics
```

### Training Needs Assessment Workflow
```
1. Collect data from multiple sources:
   - Ticket analysis (common issues, escalation causes)
   - QA findings (skill gaps, quality issues)
   - Performance reviews (individual development needs)
   - Agent feedback (self-identified gaps)
   - Customer complaints (training-related themes)
2. Analyze data to identify patterns
3. Prioritize needs by impact and frequency
4. Develop training plan to address gaps
5. Submit plan for management approval
6. Schedule training delivery
7. Deliver and evaluate training
8. Measure impact on identified metrics
```
